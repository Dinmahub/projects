1. Project Title & Company Review

MavTech Electronic: Developing an Intelligent Social Media Monitoring System to Strengthen MavTech's Brand Reputation.
INDUSTRY: Consumer Electronic Industry.
MavTech Electronics Ltd. is a leading player in the global consumer electronics industry, widely recognized for its innovation in smartphones, tablets, and wearable technology.
It has its headquarters in the United States and has built a reputation for blending cutting-edge technology with superior quality. With a dedicated workforce of 200 employees, MavTech continues to push the boundaries of product excellence and user experience.

ROLE: Senior Data Analyst.

2. Business Problem

MavTech Electronics Ltd. has been facing a growing challenge related to its brand reputation. In recent years, the company has faced several issues, including negative customer reviews, product recalls, and public relations crises. These issues have led to a decline in customer trust and have affected sales and market share.
- Negative Social Media Buzz: The company is experiencing a surge in negative social media conversations about its products and customer service. These conversations are damaging its brand image.
- Customer Complaints: Complaints related to product defects, delays in customer support response, and billing issues have been rising, leading to a drop in customer satisfaction.
- Product Recalls: Recent product recalls have received significant media attention, causing panic among customers and stakeholders.

3. Business Objectives

- Protecting Market Position: Maintaining a positive brand reputation is vital to retaining market share and fending off competition.
- Customer Retention: Improving customer satisfaction and addressing concerns promptly can reduce customer churn.
- Data-Driven Decision Making: Social media monitoring tools provide valuable insights into customer sentiment, enabling data-driven decision-making.
- Enhanced Marketing: Consistent messaging and understanding customer preferences can lead to more effective marketing campaigns.
- Crisis Mitigation: Effective monitoring can help identify and address potential crises before they escalate.

4. My Approach

- Data Collection & Preparation.
- Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA).
- Standard Query Language for Insight Generation.
- Reporting & Visualization.
- Recommendations.

TOOL USED: PostgreSQL & Power BI.

Delivered a Power BI dashboard to improve data-driven decision-making.

7. Recommendations

- Since the Elder (>60yrs) is the customer age group that generated the highest revenue, make sure the product development is user-friendly, e.g Large display and simple user interface.
- Tailor your advertisement to the Elder customers and mention the great feature that can cater for their needs.
- Give incentives to the Returning customers in order to secure their loyalty to the MavTech brand.
- Make sure laptops and smartphones are always in stock, since they are the most sort out products.
- Find out if it is a seasonal influence that causes the high increase in revenue in a specific month, and see if it can be replicated in other months that are performing low in revenue.
- The marketing campaign should be tailored to regions that are performing low in revenue in order to boost sales in all regions.
- Carry out a follow-up on the customers who mentioned the MavTech brand.
- Investigate the unique features of other competitors and see if they can be incorporated into MavTech to prevent your customers from being captivated by your competitors.
- Carry out corrective measures on the neutral and negative sentiment that customers expressed about MavTech.
- In months where we had high engagement comments, carry out an investigation as to the reason. Was it as a result of an issue that was reported? Cross-reference it via the platform in which the comments were communicated.
- Build on the Users who have above-average influencer scores and, with respect to regions, can help build Brand visibility faster, which will be welcomed easily by other potential customers.
- MavTech needs to ensure its product is in good working order before releasing it to the market, as more products are being recalled.
- Also, find out why we have more unfixed products than fixed ones. Does it have anything to do with getting the right spare parts or the lack of technical personnel (Unskilled professional)?
- Although MavTech Brand was mentioned more often, an investigation must be carried out as to the reason we have more Unhappy customers compared to Loyal customers.
