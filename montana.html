1. Project Title & Company Review

Montana Express Cafe: Sale Performance Analysis.
INDUSTRY: Food & Beverage.
Welcome to Montana Express Cafe, a new and exciting virtual café chain! The café specializes in offering a variety of drinks, snacks, and light meals to customers. Over the past year, Montana Express Cafe has seen impressive growth but now faces new challenges as the company is scaling to meet customer demand. The café has multiple locations across the city, each with its own performance metrics and unique customer preferences.

ROLE: Sales Analyst.

2. Business Problem

The Company was facing key challenges should as:
- Which menu items are performing the best across different locations?
- What is the customer profile that tends to spend the most (age, gender or preferred product?)
- How can the Cafe improve its stock management to avoid running out of popular items while also reducing overstock?
- Are there any locations or employee performance issues that require attention?

3. Business Objectives

- Find the most top-selling product that generate the most revenue.
- Find the customer demographic generating the highest revenue.
- Identify which location or employees performing above or below the average sales target.
- Analyze customer feedback to identify common themes in satisfaction or dissatisfaction.

4. My Approach

- Data Collection & Cleaning.
- Data Analysis & Insight Generation.
- Dashboard Building.
- Recommendations.

TOOL USED: MS Excel.

5. Key Deliverables

Delivered a MS Excel dashboard to improve data-driven decision-making

6. Insights

Using Root Cause Analysis for Operational Efficiency:
- Why is Montana Express having a scaling issue?
-Why are they short of stock of several products?

Using Root Cause Analysis for Customer Experience:
- Why isthe workforce not effectively trained to meet customer's needs?
- Why are there inconsistent customer rating?

7. Recommendations

- Have qualified and well trained staffs to attend to customer expectation.
- Implore the use of an inventory control and management software.
- Introduce good customer incentives to promote more sales across other locations.
- Employ good advertising medium to promote other locations and focus on the male demographic to drive more sales.
- Improve supply chain delivery timeline for efficient stocking.
